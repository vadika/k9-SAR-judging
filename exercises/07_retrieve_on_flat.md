# Exercise 7 — Retrieve on the Flat

**Applies to:** RH – F, FL, T, L, MT; Level **A** and **B**  
**Points:** 10 p

## Retrieve Article
- Utility articles, max shoe size
- Dimensions (recommended tolerances): **10–25 cm × 5–10 cm × 3–6 cm**
- Weight: **200–500 g**
- Materials: wood, leather, light metal, textile, plastic, or combination
- **NOT allowed:** jute rolls, toys, designed/fabricated articles (e.g., wrapped gloves), glass, aerosol cans, pressurised articles, plastic containers
- Articles differing from specification may be used with **TJ permission**

## Level A vs. B
- **Level A:** DH chooses article from organiser-provided selection.
- **Level B:** TJ selects one article at start of event — **same article for all participants**.

## Permitted AC/VC
- 1 AC or VC for the retrieve
- 1 AC or VC to release the article
- 1 AC or VC for final GS

## Execution
1. DH gets article shortly before exercise at designated place, returns it there after.
2. From GS (off-leash), DH throws article ~10 steps away.
3. **AC to retrieve is given only when the article comes to rest.**
4. D runs quickly to article, picks up immediately, returns directly at same pace, sits in front of DH holding the article.
5. DH takes article with 1 AC or VC; D stays close.
6. After ~3 sec, DH ends with GS.

## Evaluation — Faults
- Running slowly or not directly
- Mistakes when picking up
- Slow or indirect return
- Dropping the article
- Playing with or mouthing the article
- Throwing the article too short or too far
- Any help by the DH
