# Exercise 13 — Horizontal Ladder

**Applies to:** RH – F, FL, T, MT; Level **A** and **B**  
**Points:** 10 p

## Obstacle Spec
- Beam ladder: length **4 m**, rung distance **30 cm** (centered), rung width **5 cm**, **14 rungs**
- Padding at start and end: height ~**0.50 m**
- Mounting ramp: length **1.20 m**, width **0.50 m**; cross struts permitted as mounting aids.
- Ramp must be solid and stable.

## Permitted AC/VC
- 1 AC and/or VC for "mounting"
- 1 AC or VC for GS (at end)

## Execution

**Level A:**
1. RDT assumes GS in front of apparatus (D off-leash).
2. At AC/VC: D goes up ramp onto ladder and **independently** reaches the last rung, remaining there.
3. As soon as D reaches first rung with front paws, DH walks alongside the working D — **without touching the dog or the apparatus**.
4. At the end of the ladder, **DH lifts D off** and GS is formed.

**Level B:**
1. RDT assumes GS in front of apparatus (D off-leash).
2. At AC/VC: D goes up ramp onto ladder.
3. DH **stays** until D has reached the last rung with front paws.
4. At TJ instruction, DH walks to the waiting D.
5. **DH lifts D off** and GS is formed.

## Evaluation — Critical Rules
- **D jumps off within first half → 0 points**
- **D jumps off after first half → Insufficient**
- **D jumps off after last rung → maximum Satisfactory**
- **D falls between rungs:** if D recovers independently, scored as Insufficient; if D cannot recover, treated as jump-off at that point
