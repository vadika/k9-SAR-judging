# Exercise 1 — Reporting In and Social Behaviour

**Applies to:** All UO/GW tests (V, A, B)  
**Points:** 10 p (V) / 5 p (A, B)

## Setup
- Two circles: inner radius 2.0 m (group stays inside), outer radius 3.0 m (DH walks along outer).
- Group: exactly **3 persons**, each with a dog on leash.
- D remains **on leash** throughout.

## Permitted AC/VC
- 1 AC or VC for "Heeling" (each start)
- 1 AC or VC for microchip check

## Execution
1. Two RHTs go to the registration area at TJ/TC instruction.
2. On-leashed dogs take GS independently.
3. Register; microchip is checked.
4. Social behaviour in the group is checked.
5. One dog serves as group dog, the other is the tested dog (then they swap roles).
6. DH moves along the outer circle; group remains in inner circle (~1 m distance).

## Evaluation
- D must prove **social abilities** in the group.
- Lack of concentration and additional AC/VC devalue accordingly.
- **Aggression + attack on group dog or person → disqualification.**
