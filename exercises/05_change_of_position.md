# Exercise 5 — Change of Position

**Applies to:** RH – F, FL, T, L, MT, W; Level **V**  
**Points:** 15 p

## Testing Area
- **F, FL, T, MT:** table, 100×100 cm, height 60 cm
- **L:** snow block, natural elevation, or table (100×100 cm, 60 cm)
- **W:** surfboard or paddle board also permitted

## Permitted AC/VC
- 1 AC and/or VC to jump up and stay
- 1 AC and/or VC for each position (Sit / Down / Stand)
- 1 AC or VC for the recall
- 1 AC or VC for GS

## Execution
1. RDT assumes GS 10 steps from the table.
2. DH sends D with AC/VC to jump onto the table.
3. D jumps **without hesitation** and remains.
4. At TJ instruction to proceed: **DH decides the sequence** of positions: Sit / Down / Stand (any order).
5. DH decides whether to stand next to or move away from the table (distance not prescribed per spec — DH's call).
6. After all positions: DH recalls D with 1 AC or VC. D comes and sits in front. GS ends exercise.

## Evaluation — Faults
- Position not shown, wrong, or D does not remain in place after mounting:
  - **F, FL, T, L, MT: −4 points per fault**
  - **W: −3 points per fault**
- **D jumps off table prematurely → Insufficient (0 for that partial)**
- Hesitant mounting
- Slow recall
- Mistakes in front-sit and GS ending
