# Exercise 9 — Down Under Distraction

**Applies to:** RH – F, FL, T, L, MT, W; all levels  
**Points:** 10 p

## Setup
- Two spots marked by a line (for male and female D).
- Distance between down spot and obstacles: **minimum 10 m**.
- Distance between the two dogs (m/f): **minimum 6 m**.

## Permitted AC/VC
- 1 AC or VC for "Down"
- 1 AC or VC for "Sit" (ending)

## Execution — Common Start
1. After "Reporting in and social behaviour" and **before** the 2nd dog's UO/GW begins.
2. DH takes off-leash D to GS at the marked down area.
3. At TJ instruction: DH downs D with 1 AC/VC — **no article left with D**.
4. DH walks away (distance depends on level — see below).
5. D lies calmly without DH influence while the 2nd dog works.
6. At TJ instruction: DH **returns to D, stands to the right of D**.
7. At TJ instruction: DH gives AC/VC to sit — D sits quickly and straight. GS.

## Level Differences

| | Level V | Level A | Level B |
|---|---|---|---|
| DH position | 20 steps away, **facing D** | 40 paces away, **back to D** | **Out of D's sight** (screening wall or similar) |
| D must hold during | Ex. **2–4** (W: **3–4**) | Ex. **2–9** (L: **2–6**; W: **3–5**) | Ex. **2–9** (L: **2–6**; W: **3–5**) |
| After down period | DH stays with D at down area; **D may be leashed** | DH ends exercise at TJ instruction | DH ends exercise at TJ instruction |

## Leaving the Down — Thresholds

**Level V:**
- D leaves >3 m **before** end of exercise 2 (of 2nd RDT) → **0 points**
- D leaves >3 m **after** exercise 2 ends → **Insufficient**

**Level A and B:**
- D leaves >3 m **before** end of exercise 4 (of 2nd RDT) (L, W: exercise 3) → **0 points**
- D leaves >3 m **after** exercise 4 ends (L, W: exercise 3) → **Insufficient**

## Evaluation — Faults
- **D stands or sits but stays in down area → −5 points**
- Restless lying with lack of concentration → devalue
- Approaching DH during pickup → devalue
- Any DH influence (body language, movement) → devalue
- Additional AC/VC or body help by DH → devalue
