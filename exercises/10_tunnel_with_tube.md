# Exercise 10 — Tunnel with Tube

**Applies to:** RH – F, FL, T, MT; Level **V**, **A**, and **B**  
**Points:** 5 p (A, B) / part of V total

## Obstacle Spec
- Stiff entrance, diameter **0.50 m**, length **3 m**
- Tube at exit: soft material, length **3 m**

## Permitted AC/VC
- 1 AC and/or VC for "go through"
- 1 AC or VC for "stay" (after exit)
- 1 AC or VC for GS

## Execution
1. RDT assumes GS in front of obstacle (D off-leash).
2. At AC/VC for "through": D goes **confidently** through tunnel.
3. After D exits tunnel: DH gives AC/VC to "stay".
4. At TJ instruction: DH goes to D, ends exercise with AC or VC for GS.

## Evaluation — Faults
- Insecure, hesitant, and/or hectic going in or through → devalue
- **D does not leave tunnel → Insufficient**
- **D does not stay after AC/VC → Insufficient**
