# Exercise 8 — Directability at Distance

**Applies to:** RH – F, FL, T, L, MT; all levels  
**Points:** 10 p (V) / 15 p (A, B for F, FL, T, MT) / **20 p (A, B for L)**

## Testing Area
- **F, FL, T, MT:** tables
- **L:** backpacks/buckets
- **Level V:** 1 marked start point + 2 tables/backpacks 40 m apart and 40 m from start (triangle)
- **Level A/B:** 1 marked middle + 3 tables/backpacks at prescribed distances

## Permitted AC/VC — Level V
- 1 AC and/or VC to go to each marked point
- 1 AC and/or VC for remaining in place
- 1 AC or VC for recall
- 1 AC or VC for GS

## Permitted AC/VC — Level A and B
- 1 AC and/or VC for "going out to the middle marker"
- 1 AC or VC for "remaining in place"
- 1 AC and/or VC for running to each table/backpack
- 1 AC for jumping on and remaining (or remaining at backpack — L)
- 1 AC or VC for recall
- 1 AC or VC for GS

## Execution (Level A/B)
1. DH sends D to the **middle** marker first.
2. D remains at middle.
3. DH sends D to 1st table (in drawn sequence) — D jumps on (or runs to — L) and remains.
4. Same for 2nd and 3rd table.
5. D recalled from 3rd table; comes quickly, sits in front; GS.

## Elimination
- **Middle not shown → Insufficient**
- **Drawn sequence of tables not followed → Insufficient**

## Evaluation — Faults
- Hesitant running to tables/zones
- Strongly deviating from ideal line
- Hesitant jumping/remaining
- DH leaves designated position
