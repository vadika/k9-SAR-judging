# Exercise 4 — Distance Control

**Applies to:** RH – F, FL, T, L, MT, W; Level **A** and **B**  
**Points:** 15 p

## Sequences
- **Level A:** Sit → Recall → Down → Recall
- **Level B:** Sit → Recall → Down → Stand → Recall

## Permitted AC/VC
| Level A | Level B |
|---|---|
| 1 AC for "heel" | 1 AC and/or VC for "stand" |
| 1 AC or VC for "sit" | 1 AC or VS for "recall" |
| 1 AC or VC for "recall" | 1 AC or VC for GS |
| 1 AC or VC for "down" | 1 AC or VC for "down" |
| 1 AC or VC for GS | 1 AC or VC for "sit" |

## Execution
**Phase 1:** From GS, DH walks off-leash D straight. After 10–15 steps, D **sits** at 1 AC/VC — DH does NOT break stride or turn.

After ~40 steps, DH stops and turns to face D.

**Phase 2:** At TJ instruction, DH recalls D with 1 AC/VC. D runs toward DH happily and fast. **At the midpoint of the distance**, DH gives 1 AC/VC for "down" — D must drop immediately. D stays in down.

**Level B addition:** At TJ instruction, D **stands** at 1 AC/VC. D stays standing.

**Phase 3:** At TJ instruction, DH calls D with 1 AC/VC. D comes quickly, sits close and straight in front. After ~3 sec, DH orders GS.

> The down (and stand in B) happens **while the dog is in motion toward DH**, not after it arrives.

## Rule: Distance Violation
- If the D surpasses the designated distance for the 1st recall / 2nd position by more than **10 steps** → partial exercise maximum **Satisfactory**.

## Partial Point Breakdown
| Part | F, FL, T, MT | W |
|---|---|---|
| Part 1: Initial GS, development, sit & remain | 6 p | 4 p |
| Part 2: Approach and assume & remain "down" | 5 p | 3 p |
| Part 3 (A): Approach, front sit, GS | 4 p | 3 p |
| Part 3 (B): Stand, approach, front sit, GS | 4 p | 3 p |
