# Exercise 3 — Sit in Motion with Recall

**Applies to:** RH – F, FL, T, L, MT; Level **V**  
**Points:** 10 p

## Permitted AC/VC
- 1 AC or VC for "Heeling"
- 1 AC or VC for "Sit"
- 1 AC or VC for "Recall"
- 1 AC or VC for "GS"

## Execution
**Phase 1:** From GS, DH walks with off-leash D straight on. After 10–15 steps, D sits **in the direction of movement** on AC/VC for "sit" — DH must NOT break stride or turn around.

**Phase 2:** DH walks approx. 30 more steps straight, then stops and turns to face D.

**Phase 3:** At TJ instruction, DH recalls D with 1 AC or VC. D comes quickly and sits close and straight in front. After ~3 sec, DH ends exercise with GS.

## Evaluation — Faults
- **Wrong position after "sit" command (down or stand): −5 points**
- Mistakes in development (heeling phase)
- Slow or unsteady sit
- Slow recall
- Mistakes in front-sit before DH
- Mistakes in ending (GS)
- Additional AC or body aids by DH
