# Exercise 2 — On-Leash Heeling (V) / Off-Leash Heeling (A, B)

**Applies to:** All RH types, all levels  
**Points:** 15 p (V on-leash) / 10 p (A, B off-leash)

## Permitted AC/VC
- 1 short AC or VC to heel — may be given **at each start and change of pace**

## Execution
- TJ gives directive to start. All subsequent turns, halts, pace changes are done **without TJ directive**.
- Begins: RDT goes 50 steps straight on the middle line at normal pace without stopping.
- After about-turn: continues, including changes of pace (slow, fast, normal).
- Includes right turns (RW), left turns (LW), and u-turns (KW).
- At each halt: D must sit independently.
- On-leash: leash must remain loose.
- Off-leash: D heels freely, leash not attached.

## Evaluation — Faults
- Forging (ahead of DH)
- Deviating sideways
- Lagging
- Slow or hesitant sitting at halts
- Strained leash (on-leash only)
- Submissive behaviour of D
- Additional AC/VC by DH
- Body help by DH
