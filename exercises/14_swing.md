# Exercise 14 — Swing

**Applies to:** RH – F, FL, T, MT; Level **B** only  
**Points:** 10 p

## Obstacle Spec
- Movable wooden plank: length ~**3.0 m**, width ~**30 cm**
- Support construction at start and end with mechanism to affix plank: **40 cm from ground**
- Mounting/dismounting: 2 wooden boards, length ~**1.5 m**, width ~**30 cm**
- Similar constructions with same measurements and movability are admitted.

## Permitted AC/VC
- 1 AC and/or VC for "mounting"
- 1 AC for "staying" (pause on plank)
- 1 AC or VC for each "go on"

## Execution
1. RDT assumes GS in front of swing (D off-leash).
2. At AC/VC to "mount": D steps onto movable plank via mounting ramp.
3. At AC: D **immediately stays**.
4. At TJ directive: DH walks to D's side, gives AC/VC to "go on", walks alongside D to end.
5. D dismounts via dismounting ramp; GS is formed.

## Evaluation — Critical Rules
- **D jumps off within first half → 0 points**
- **D jumps off after first half → Insufficient**
- Insecure / hectic behaviour or insufficient coordination → devalue
- Hesitant or hectic mounting → devalue
- Insecure, anxious, erratic traversal → devalue
- Running ahead / lagging behind → devalue
