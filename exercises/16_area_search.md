# Area Search (RH – FL)

## General Rules
- DH may **not run** — normal gait only.
- DH gets detailed sketch showing search area borders.
- DH must **not** walk around the area perimeter or along borders; **minimum distance from border: 30 m**.
- DH must select tactic **before** deploying D; announces to TJ.
- If tactical changes occur during work: notify TJ; search time continues.
- If DH finds VP: no restriction if D continues searching freely and alerts correctly.
- Before sending D, DH announces: continue to start point (where alert was reported) OR continue search.

## Victims (VP) Placement
- Clothing **must not stand out** (no reflective or striking colours).
- Minimum **20 m** apart.
- Must **lie down or sit**; **face must be uncovered** — no full coverage with tarps or blankets.
- Remain silent and still; cannot help D or DH in any way.
- VP leaves hide as soon as found, at TJ instruction (including removing mats/padding).
- Used hides may be reused for subsequent dogs.

**Max:** 100 points

| Component | Points |
|---|---|
| Work mode in search | 30 p |
| Alert at person | 70 p |

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Search area | 5,000 m² open and covered terrain |
| Victims | 1 VP |
| Hides | VP must have visual and physical contact with D |
| Time | max 10 minutes |

**Evaluation:**
- VP not found → **cannot pass**
- 1st false alert: **−20 points**
- Evaluated especially: teamwork DH–D

- After alert exercise, DH goes to start position; TJ announces tactical situation.

- Systematic search of area is key.
- **1st false alert: −40 points**
- VP not found → **cannot pass**; max achievable = **139 points**

## FL B — Area Search B

**Max:** 200 points

| Component | Points |
|---|---|
| Alert exercise | 10 p |
| Directability | 15 p |
| Search Intensity | 15 p |
| Mobility | 10 p |
| Independence | 10 p |
| Tactic & Teamwork | 20 p |
| Alerts (3 persons × 40 p each) | 120 p |

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Search area | 40,000 m² (100 × 400 m) |
| Terrain | Min 50% covered, limited overview; may contain individual buildings |
| Victims | 3 VP |
| Hides | Visual and/or physical contact; typical terrain hides (e.g., elevated hides) permitted |
| Time | max 30 minutes |

**Execution:**
- D searches with **deep alternating side sweeps**.
- DH moves **only along the middle line** of the search area (except at the alert).
- Elevated hides permitted but must be **min 20 m inside** area boundaries; max **3 m** height.

**Evaluation:**
- Systematic search.
- **1st false alert: −40 points**
- VP not found → **cannot pass**; max achievable = **139 points**
