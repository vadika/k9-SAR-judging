# Abbreviations

## Roles and Participants

| Abbr | Meaning |
|---|---|
| D | Dog |
| DH | Dog Handler |
| TJ | Trial Judge |
| TC | Trial Chairman |
| HP | Hilfsperson — assistant in the Carry and Hand-over exercise |
| VP | Versuchsperson — the victim person being searched for |
| FHL | Fährtenleger — track layer (F discipline) |
| TL | Trail Layer — person being trailed (MT discipline); also used as Team Leader in general SAR context |
| RHT | RettungsHundTeam — Rescue Dog Team (DH + D together) |
| RDT | Rescue Dog Team — same as RHT; both appear in the documents |

## Notes

- **VS** appears once in the exercise files (Ex. 4 Distance Control) where **VC** is clearly intended — likely a transcription error in the source document.
- **TL** is overloaded: Trail Layer in MT nosework context; Team Leader in general organizational context. In the alert rules table ("Any assistance from DH/VP/TL → 0 points"), TL refers to the person being searched for in the MT discipline.
- **RHT / RDT**: both mean the working team (DH + D). RHT is the German-derived form, RDT is the English rendering. Used interchangeably in the documents.
