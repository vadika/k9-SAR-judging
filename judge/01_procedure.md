# Procedure — Reporting In/Out, Between Exercises

## Before the First Exercise

DH **reports in to the TJ** before the first exercise — leashed D in GS, leash ~1 m, collar. Procedural, not scored.
- Scorebook (LH) must be handed over before the test.
- DH declares D's alert type to TJ (nosework).
- Gear violations are caught here.

## After the Last Exercise

DH reports out to TJ — leashed D in GS. Procedural, not scored.

---

## Temperament Observation

The TJ **actively observes D's temperament prior to beginning and during the entire test**. Explicit obligation.

- Obvious inadequacies (aggression toward strangers/other dogs, uncontrollable leaving of DH) → **termination**
- Aggressive behaviour → **disqualification**

Applies from arrival, including before Reporting In.

---

## Between Exercises

- No separate scoring phase exists between exercises.
- The **GS at start and end of each exercise** is formally part of that exercise.
- The **Development** criterion applies whenever D is moving alongside DH.
- Temperament observation continues — a dog that acts out between exercises can still trigger termination/disqualification.
- **Down Under Distraction** is deliberately placed to run *during* the other dog's UO/GW — it is the one scored element that occupies the inter-exercise window.
