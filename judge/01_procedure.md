# Procedure — Reporting In/Out, Leash Rules, Between Exercises

## Before the First Exercise

DH **reports in to the TJ** before the first exercise — leashed D in GS, leash ~1 m, collar. Procedural, not scored.
- Scorebook (LH) must be handed over before the test.
- DH declares D's alert type to TJ (nosework).
- Gear violations are caught here.

## After the Last Exercise

DH reports out to TJ — leashed D in GS. Procedural, not scored.

---

## Leash Rules — Full Map

### UO/GW

| Moment | Leash status |
|---|---|
| Formal reporting in to TJ (before first exercise) | ON LEASH, ~1 m, collar |
| Reporting In exercise (Ex. 1) | ON LEASH — all levels, explicit |
| Heeling Level V | ON LEASH |
| Heeling Level A/B | OFF LEASH |
| All other UO/GW exercises | OFF LEASH |
| Down Under Distraction setup (moving to down spot) | OFF LEASH — explicitly stated |
| Formal reporting out after last exercise | ON LEASH |

### Nosework — Reporting In to TJ

All disciplines: ON LEASH in GS before each search discipline.

### Nosework — During Search and Between VPs

| Discipline | During search | Between VPs |
|---|---|---|
| Area (FL) | Off leash | Off leash — explicitly: *"goes back with his off-leashed D"* |
| Rubble (T) | Off leash, **no collar** (except bringsel) | No leash possible; work continues at TJ instruction |
| Tracking (F) | DH's choice: off-leash or 10 m leash (collar or harness) | Continuous — reports back ON LEASH after track, before alert exercise |
| Avalanche (L) | Off leash | Off leash; work continues without pause |
| Mantrailing (MT) | ON LEASH, 5–10 m, search harness | Continuous trail; leash handling explicitly evaluated |

### Rubble Special Case — Pre-Search Perimeter Walk

During the DH's due-diligence walk around the perimeter (before announcing tactic), D must remain at a designated spot *"either freely or tied down"* — DH's choice. This time counts against the search clock.

---

## Temperament Observation

The TJ **actively observes D's temperament prior to beginning and during the entire test**. Explicit obligation.

- Obvious inadequacies (aggression toward strangers/other dogs, uncontrollable leaving of DH) → **termination**
- Aggressive behaviour → **disqualification**

Applies from arrival, including before Reporting In.

---

## Between Exercises

- No separate scoring phase exists between exercises.
- The **GS at start and end of each exercise** is formally part of that exercise.
- The **Development** criterion applies whenever D is moving alongside DH.
- Temperament observation continues — a dog that acts out between exercises can still trigger termination/disqualification.
- **Down Under Distraction** is deliberately placed to run *during* the other dog's UO/GW — it is the one scored element that occupies the inter-exercise window.
